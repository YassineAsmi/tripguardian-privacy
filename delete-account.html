Delete Your TripGuardian Account

If you would like to permanently delete your TripGuardian account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to supp.tripguardian@gmail.com from the email address linked to your TripGuardian account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. In your message, please confirm your full name and any group/guide association (if applicable).
